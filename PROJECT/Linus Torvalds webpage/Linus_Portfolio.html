Biography

Linus Torvalds is Finnish-American software engineer who is the creator and principal developer of the Linux kernal, which is the for a number of operating system.He also Created the version control system Git.Torvals was born on December 28, 1969 in Helsinki, Finland. He studied computer science at the University of Helsinki, and he developed the Linux kernal as as student project u=in 1991.Since then, the Linux kernal has become has become one of the most widely used kernals in the world, powering everything from smartphones to supercomputers.
